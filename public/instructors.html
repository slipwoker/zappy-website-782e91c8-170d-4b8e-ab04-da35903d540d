Meet Your Guides

At Stillwater, our teaching philosophy is rooted in warmth, attentiveness, and personal guidance. We believe that yoga is not a one-size-fits-all practice, but a deeply individual journey. Our instructors are dedicated to creating a safe, nurturing space where every student—from first-timers to seasoned practitioners—feels seen, supported, and empowered to explore their edge with kindness.

With a focus on mindful alignment and breath-led movement, we offer more than just a class; we offer a partnership in your wellness. Our team combines decades of collective experience with a genuine passion for the transformative power of stillness, ensuring that your time on the mat translates into a more balanced life off it.

Meet Your Guides

Our instructors bring warmth, deep knowledge, and a genuine love for sharing the practice. Each one creates a space where you can feel safe, seen, and supported on your mat.

Maya

Her classes weave slow, intentional movement with guided breathwork, inviting you to drop out of the mind and into the body. Our Teaching Philosophy

At Stillwater, our practice extends beyond the mat. We believe in creating a sanctuary where mindful pacing, inclusive guidance, and intentional stillness converge to help you discover your own path to wellness.

Mindful Pacing

We move with intention, not urgency. Every class is thoughtfully sequenced to honor the body's natural rhythm, allowing you to build strength and flexibility at a pace that feels deeply restorative rather than rushed.

Inclusive Guidance

Yoga is for every body. Our instructors offer personalized modifications and compassionate cues that meet you exactly where you are, ensuring that every student feels seen, supported, and empowered in their practice.

Space for Stillness

In a world that demands constant movement, we protect the pause. We integrate generous moments of meditation and breathwork into every session, cultivating a profound sense of inner quiet that you can carry beyond the studio walls.
